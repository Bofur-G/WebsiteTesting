Policies

Disclaimer:

Employees are cautioned that the Internet includes offensive, sexually explicit, and other questionable material. Even innocuous search requests may lead to sites with offensive content. Additionally, having an email address on the Internet may lead to receipt of unsolicited email containing offensive content. Employees accessing email and the Internet do so at their own risk and Garland is not responsible for material viewed or downloaded by employees. To minimize these risks, your use of email and Internet on Garland’s Computer Network (“Computer Network”) and/or on Garland’s Email System (“Email System”) is governed by the following policy:


Garland’s Computer Network:

Responsible Use: The Computer Network is the property of Garland. The Computer Network is designed to assist employees in conducting business within and for Garland. All employees have a responsibility to use Garland’s computer resources and the Internet in a professional, lawful, and ethical manner. Abuse of the Computer Network, email, instant messaging, or the Internet, may result in disciplinary action including possible termination and/or criminal prosecution.

Personal Use: Occasional limited personal use of the computer is permitted if such use does not a) interfere with any employee’s job performance; b) damage the computer or Computer Network’s performance; or c) violate any other policies, provisions, guidelines or standards of Garland. Personal use of the computer is a privilege that may be revoked at any time.






No expectation of privacy


Garland’s Right to Review: The Computer Network is the property of Garland. Employees should have no expectation of privacy in anything they create, delete, store, send or receive using the Computer Network or Garland’s computer equipment. Garland reserves the right to review, audit, monitor and access all materials created, stored, sent or received by employees through the Computer Network or any Internet connection, or stored on Garland’s computer equipment without the permission of employees. Employees expressly waive any right of privacy in anything they create, store, send or receive using Garland’s computer equipment, the Computer Network, the Email System, instant messaging or Internet access.

Confidentiality: The confidentiality of any email message should not be assumed. Even when a message is erased, it is still possible to retrieve and read that message. Further, the use of passwords for security does not guarantee confidentiality. Email messages should be treated as confidential by other employees and accessed only by the intended recipient. Without specific advance authorization, employees are not authorized to retrieve or read any email messages that are not sent to them.

UNDER NO CIRCUMSTANCES is texting or making use of electronic mail functions permitted while operating a motor vehicle on behalf of Garland or in a Garland-owned or reimbursed vehicle. Additionally, you are required to use a hands-free device when using a cell phone (personal cell phone or company cell phone) while operating a motor vehicle on behalf of Garland or in a Garland-owned or reimbursed vehicle.

If there are questions concerning this policy and/or should you become aware of a violation of this policy, you should notify/contact Legal.
